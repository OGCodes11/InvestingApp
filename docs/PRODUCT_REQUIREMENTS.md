# Product Requirements (MVP)

## Goals

- Help users learn investing fundamentals.
- Enable transparent social sharing of investing activity.
- Incentivize high-quality participation through earned verification.

## User roles

- Guest: read-only public content
- Registered user: post, follow, message, enroll in courses
- Verified user: trusted badge and discovery boost
- Admin/moderator: enforce policy and review abuse reports

## Feature requirements

1. Social feed
   - Create posts with ticker tags and optional chart image
   - Like, comment, save, and follow from feed
2. Markets
   - Top stocks bought/sold over configurable windows
   - Curated external market news list
3. Courses and badges
   - Multi-lesson courses with completion tracking
   - Badge awarded per completed course
4. Verification
   - Eligible after all required badges
   - Final assessment with pass/fail outcome
5. Messaging
   - 1:1 direct message threads with near realtime updates
